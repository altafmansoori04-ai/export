Our Story

Seven Tail was founded in 2005 with a passion for bringing beautiful, handcrafted carpets to modern homes. Our artisans combine traditional techniques with contemporary designs to create unique pieces that transform living spaces.

Each carpet in our collection is carefully crafted using the finest materials, including premium wool, silk, and eco-friendly fibers. We take pride in our sustainable practices and ethical sourcing.
